Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you provide security services in?

We provide security services throughout India, with a strong presence in Dehradun, Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, and other major cities. We can also arrange services in other locations based on client requirements.

How are your security personnel trained?

All our security personnel undergo a rigorous training program that includes physical training, security protocols, emergency response, conflict resolution, and customer service. Many of our team members have military or police backgrounds.

How quickly can you arrange security for an event?

While we appreciate advance notice, we understand that security needs can arise suddenly. For standard events, we recommend booking at least 7 days in advance. For emergency situations, we can often arrange security personnel within 24 hours.

Are your security personnel licensed and insured?

Yes, all our security personnel are properly licensed according to local regulations. Our company carries comprehensive insurance coverage, providing peace of mind for our clients.
