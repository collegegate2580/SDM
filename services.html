Frequently Asked Questions

How much advance notice do you need for security services?

For optimal service, we recommend booking at least 7-14 days in advance, especially for large events. However, we understand that security needs can arise suddenly, and we can often accommodate last-minute requests depending on our current schedule and your requirements.

Are your security personnel licensed and insured?

Yes, all our security personnel are properly licensed and trained according to industry standards and local regulations. SSM Security carries comprehensive liability insurance, providing peace of mind for our clients.

Can your guards be armed if needed?

Yes, we can provide armed security personnel for situations that require it. All our armed guards have proper licensing, extensive training, and experience. This service is subject to additional requirements and regulations, which we will discuss during the consultation process.

What types of events do you provide security for?

We provide security for a wide range of events including corporate functions, weddings, concerts, festivals, sporting events, trade shows, conferences, political rallies, religious gatherings, and private parties. No event is too small or too large for our team.
